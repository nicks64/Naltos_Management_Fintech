# ✅ **EXAMPLE 1 — Rent Flow (Tenant → Property Owner)**

### ✔ **What the tenant sees:**

* Tenant logs into AppFolio
* Pays **$1,500 USD**
* Gets a confirmation: “Rent paid.”
* Sees **$3.50 cashback** in their rewards balance

No crypto, no stablecoins, nothing unusual.

### ✔ **What happens under the hood (Naltos rails):**

1. Tenant pays **$1,500 USD** → property bank account
2. Naltos converts **$1,500 USD → stablecoins (USDC/USDT/DAI)**

   * Only used as *rails*
3. Naltos mints **1,500 NUSD** internally
4. Money enters **Rent Float Bucket (10 days)**
5. Treasury deployment:

   * 75% into **T-bills** (NRF)
   * 25% into **money-market** (NRK)
6. Yield generated in 10 days:

   * **$1.23 in yield**
7. Yield distribution:

   * Owner: **$1.11 USD**
   * Tenant cashback: **$0.12 USD**
   * Naltos: included in rounding
8. On payout day:

   * NUSD → stablecoins → **USD → owner’s bank account**

### ✔ **Final state:**

* Owner receives **$1,501.11 USD**
* Tenant receives **$0.12 USD** in cashback
* Naltos earns its share of micro-yield
* All transactions: **USD**

Stablecoins only served as faster, programmable rails.

# ✅ **EXAMPLE 2 — Vendor Payment (Net30)**

### ✔ **What vendor sees:**

* Vendor sends invoice for **$10,000 USD**
* Usually waits 30 days
* With Naltos → gets **$10,000 USD instantly**
* Sees a note: “Paid early — Net30 auto-settlement scheduled.”

Everything in **USD**.

### ✔ **Backend (Naltos rails):**

1. Vendor issues **$10k USD invoice**

2. PM approves in Buildium

3. Naltos immediately pays vendor **$10k USD-equivalent**

   * Vendor sees USD balance

4. Under the hood:

   * Naltos converts $10k USD → stablecoin rails
   * Mints 10k NUSD

5. Treasury float window = **30 days**

6. Treasury deployment:

   * 50% → NRF (T-bills)
   * 30% → NRK (money market)
   * 20% → NRC (delta-neutral credit)

7. Yield generated in 30 days:

   * **$37.50 USD**

8. Yield split:

   * Property owner: **$33.75 USD**
   * Vendor cashback: **$1.88 USD**
   * Naltos: **$1.88 USD**

9. On day 30:

   * Vendor’s USD payout is processed through ACH
   * NUSD → stablecoins → USD → Vendor bank

### ✔ **Final state:**

* Vendor → gets **full $10,000 USD**
* Property earns **$33.75 USD extra yield**
* Vendor earns **$1.88 USD cashback**
* Platform earns **$1.88 USD**
* Everything is **in USD**

Stablecoins were simply transport rails.

# ✅ **EXAMPLE 3 — Merchant Transaction + P2P Flow**

This shows tenant → merchant → tenant, and how USD continues to be USD.

### ✔ **Merchant Purchase (Tenant → Merchant)**

1. Tenant buys food at clubhouse cafe for **$20 USD**
2. Merchant sees:
   **“Payment received: $20 USD”**

### Backend Rails

1. Naltos updates ledger
2. Converts small USD amounts → stablecoins to settle instantly
3. Mints NUSD internally
4. Merchant settlement window = 2 days
5. NUSD sits in **Micro Float Bucket**

Yield generated (example):

* 2-day float → **$0.006 USD**

Split:

* Property Owner (50%) → **$0.003**
* Tenant (40%) → **$0.0024 cashback**
* Naltos (10%) → **$0.0006**

### Merchant Settlement

* Merchant settles weekly
* Receives **USD** to their bank

Everything denominated in **USD**.

# ✔ **P2P Example (Tenant → Roommate in USD)**

Tenant sends **$50 USD** to roommate.

### Tenant sees:

* “$50 sent.”

### Roommate sees:

* “$50 received.”

### Backend:

1. Internal NUSD ledger updated
2. If needed, stablecoin rails facilitate instant reconciliation
3. NUSD balances sit in liquidity bucket
4. Idle USD creates micro-float → yields pocket change
5. All balances are redeemed in USD if final settlement needed
