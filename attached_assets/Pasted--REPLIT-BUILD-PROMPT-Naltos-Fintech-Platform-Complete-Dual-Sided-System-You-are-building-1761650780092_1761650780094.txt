🚀 REPLIT BUILD PROMPT — “Naltos Fintech Platform” (Complete Dual-Sided System)

You are building a production-quality fintech platform called Naltos, designed for the multifamily real estate sector.
The platform has two fully separate experiences:
    1.    Business Side (Property Managers / Owners)
    2.    Consumer Side (Tenants / Residents)

Both sides connect to Naltos’ AI-driven Treasury system, which allocates platform float and deposits into short-duration, dual-collateral instruments (similar to STRK/STRF/STRC) to generate returns for both parties.

⸻

🏗️ 0. Tech Foundation
    •    Stack: TypeScript, Next.js 14, Tailwind CSS, Prisma + SQLite (mock DB), Node/Express or Next API Routes.
    •    Include authentication with Login / Signup / Logout and role-based access (tenant, PM, admin, CFO).
    •    All data sources are mocked but consistent (fixtures and generated values).
    •    Include a dark/light mode, audit logs, and a Reset Demo button.

⸻

🧭 1. Platform Overview

Naltos is a fintech infrastructure platform for multifamily operators that:
    •    Automates rent collection, reconciliation, liquidity management, and financial reporting.
    •    Uses agentic AI to analyze data, converse with users, and automate decisions.
    •    Generates returns from idle balances and float via Naltos Reserve Instruments:
    •    NRF (Reserve Fund) → short-term Treasury-backed.
    •    NRK (Reserve Token) → tokenized T-Bills.
    •    NRC (Reserve Cash+) → dual-collateral (BTC + Treasuries), AI-managed, delta-hedged.

⸻

🏢 3. Business Dashboard (Property Managers / Owners)

Professional-grade dashboard for real estate operators.

Pages:
    1.    Overview – KPIs and summaries
    •    Metrics: On-Time %, DSO, Delinquent $, Treasury AUM, Average Yield.
    •    Graphs: NOI trend, delinquency heatmap, liquidity projection.
    2.    Collections – rent payment tracking, delinquency automation.
    •    Table: Tenant | Unit | Amount | Due | Status | Suggested Action.
    •    Buttons: Send Paylink, Auto-Nudge, Schedule Reminder.
    •    AI Agent integration: “Predict who’s most likely to pay this week.”
    3.    Reconciliation – auto-matching and anomaly detection.
    •    2-pane ledger: PMS vs. Naltos Treasury.
    •    One-click Approve Matches + “Hours Saved” metric.
    4.    Treasury – manage idle funds via Naltos Reserve.
    •    Product cards: NRF, NRK, NRC (STRF/STRK/STRC analogs).
    •    Show AUM, Yield %, OC%, Duration, Liquidity.
    •    Actions: Subscribe, Redeem, Auto-Roll, View Fact Sheet.
    5.    Reports – full analytics suite.
    •    NOI reports, delinquency trends, yield breakdowns, property-level returns.
    •    Export: CSV, PDF.
    6.    Agent – conversational AI copilot.
    •    Prompts: “Summarize this month’s rent performance,” “Forecast next quarter liquidity,” “Optimize reserve allocation.”
    7.    Settings – org details, PMS integration, compliance options.

💰 5. Treasury & Instruments (Shared Layer)

The Naltos Treasury is a unified back-end engine managing all float and reserve balances.

Product    Analog    Collateral    Target Yield    Access
NRF (Reserve Fund)    STRF    100% U.S. T-Bills / repos    5.0%    Default float
NRK (Reserve Token)    STRK    Tokenized T-Bill exposure    5.2%    Treasury clients
NRC (Reserve Cash+)    STRC    70–80% T-Bills + 20–30% BTC (hedged)    5.8–6.2%    Accredited / Business clients

Treasury Console:
    •    Graphs: Yield, OC%, AUM over time.
    •    Controls: Rebalance, Hedge, Stress Test, Optimize Yield.
    •    AI prompts:
    •    “Optimize carry while maintaining OC ≥ 1.35x.”
    •    “Stress test: -20% BTC, show liquidity coverage.”
