# Naltos Platform: Detailed Feature Catalog & Business Value Proposition

## **1. Executive Summary**
Naltos is a **Cash-Flow Intelligence and Payments Optimization Platform** specifically designed for multifamily real estate. It transforms rent collection from a passive administrative task into a strategic, value-driving operation. By integrating advanced behavioral incentives, predictive analytics, and float optimization, Naltos increases Property Net Operating Income (NOI), reduces operational overhead, and fosters a path to homeownership for residents.

---

## **2. Detailed Feature Catalog**

### **A. Business Console (Owners, CFOs, & Property Managers)**
#### **1. Rent Stability & Cash-Flow Intelligence**
*   **Portfolio Volatility Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of rent collection patterns across properties to identify liquidity risks early.
*   **Delinquency Reduction Dashboard**: Visualize the ROI of incentive programs through reduced late payments and avoided eviction costs.
*   **Predictive Payment Probability**: ML-ready scoring (0-100) for every tenant, predicting the likelihood of on-time payment based on historical behavior.
*   **NOI Uplift Analytics**: Calculates the direct financial gain from improved collection efficiency and yield optimization.

#### **2. Treasury & Float Optimization (Invisible Backend)**
*   **Rent Float Management**: Automatically earns yield on the "float" period between when a tenant pays and when funds are deployed for property expenses.
*   **Vendor Float Optimization (Net30-90)**: Property managers can authorize instant payments to vendors while Naltos maintains the cash in a yield-generating treasury for 30, 60, or 90 days.
*   **Multi-Product Treasury**: Automated deployment of idle cash into treasury-backed assets (NRF, NRK, NRC) to maximize risk-adjusted returns.
*   **Programmable Yield Distribution**: Rules-based engine that splits generated yield between the Owner (NOI), the Tenant (Incentives), and the Platform.

#### **3. Operational Automation**
*   **Internal Ledger System**: A robust, PostgreSQL-based "source of truth" for all internal accounting units (NUSD), ensuring perfect auditability.
*   **Automated Reconciliation**: High-confidence matching of bank ledger entries to internal payment records, reducing manual accounting labor by up to 70%.
*   **PMS Bridge**: Seamlessly sits on top of existing Property Management Systems (Yardi, AppFolio, Buildium) as a modern payment and intelligence layer.

### **B. Tenant Portal (Residents)**
#### **1. Behavioral Incentives & Rewards**
*   **USD Cashback Engine**: Residents earn real USD rewards for on-time payments, streaks, and early settlement—incentivizing positive behavior.
*   **Mobile-First Payment Experience**: Clean, intuitive interface for paying rent, viewing rewards, and tracking financial progress.
*   **Merchant Integration**: Allows residents to spend their earned rewards at local or partner merchants, boosting the local ecosystem.

#### **2. Path to Ownership (Ownership Readiness)**
*   **Eligibility Tiering**: Residents are categorized into FHA (Tier 2) or NACA (Tier 3) eligibility based on their payment consistency and credit score.
*   **Credit Reporting (Rent-to-Credit)**: Opt-in service that reports on-time rent payments to major credit bureaus, building a "mortgage-ready" credit profile.
*   **Ownership Roadmap**: Visual progress bars and milestone tracking for down payment savings (from yield) and credit score targets.
*   **Partner Lender Routing**: Pre-qualification routing to FHA and NACA-compliant lenders for residents who hit "Tier 3" readiness.

### **C. Vendor & Merchant Ecosystem**
*   **Instant Payment Access**: Vendors can opt into "Push-to-Card" instant payouts for a small fee, improving their own cash flow while Naltos captures the spread.
*   **Multi-Org Vendor Login**: Professional portal where a vendor can manage invoices and balances across multiple different property management companies with one login.
*   **Settlement Preferences**: Merchants can define how and when they receive funds, choosing between traditional ACH or instant digital settlement.

## **3. Business Value Proposition**

### **For Property Owners & Investors**
*   **Direct NOI Growth**: 0.5% to 1.5% increase in annual NOI through yield capture on idle float and reduced bad debt.
*   **Asset Value Appreciation**: Every dollar of NOI created adds ~$15–$20 to the property’s valuation (assuming a 5-6% cap rate).
*   **Stabilized Cash Flow**: Predictive models allow for better liquidity planning and reduce the need for short-term working capital.

### **For Property Management Companies**
*   **Reduced Labor Costs**: Automation of reconciliation and the shift from "collections" to "incentives" frees up site staff for leasing and resident retention.
*   **Competitive Differentiation**: Offering a "Path to Ownership" and "Rent Cashback" makes the property significantly more attractive to high-quality, stable residents.
*   **Lower Turnover**: Rewarded tenants are 30% more likely to renew their leases, drastically reducing the high cost of unit turns.

### **For Residents (The "Sticky" Value)**
*   **Wealth Creation**: Rent is no longer a "lost" expense; it becomes a tool for building credit and saving for a home.
*   **Financial Inclusion**: Access to institutional-grade mortgage readiness tools (NACA/FHA) previously unavailable to most renters.
*   **Immediate ROI**: Direct USD cashback provides tangible value for being a "good tenant."

## **4. Strategic Framework: The Naltos Flywheel**
1.  **Incentivize**: Better behavior (on-time rent) leads to...
2.  **Optimize**: More predictable cash flow and longer float periods, which leads to...
3.  **Monetize**: Higher yield and performance sharing, which leads to...
4.  **Reinvest**: Better rewards for tenants and higher NOI for owners, starting the cycle again.
